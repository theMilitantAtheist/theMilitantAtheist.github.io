About

Welcome to The Militant Atheists website, a website to link the social medias, and maybe, just maybe make you understand a little more what Atheism even is!

Atheism is a philosophical position that asserts the non-existence of gods or the lack of belief in their existence. As Atheists, we aim to provide resources and foster a community for individuals who embrace this perspective.

Our goal is not to attack or demean religious individuals but rather to critically examine and discuss the concepts, beliefs, and systems associated with religions.

FAQ

Q: Do atheists hate people who follow a religion?

A: No, atheism itself does not entail hatred towards individuals who follow a religion. While some atheists may have strong disagreements with certain religious beliefs or practices, it is essential to separate criticism of ideas from personal animosity towards people.

Q: Is atheism a form of religion?

A: No, atheism is not a religion. It is the absence of belief in deities. Religions typically involve belief in supernatural entities, rituals, and organized systems of worship. Atheism, on the other hand, focuses on the rejection or lack of belief in such supernatural claims.

Q: Are atheists immoral or amoral?

A: Atheism does not imply any specific moral stance. Morality is a complex and multifaceted subject that varies among individuals. Many atheists derive their moral values from humanistic principles, ethics, and empathy.
